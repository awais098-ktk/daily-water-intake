Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ moment().format('MMMM DD, YYYY') }}

1. Information We Collect

HydroMate collects the following information to provide water intake tracking services:

  • Account Information: Username, email, and profile data
  • Water Intake Data: Daily water consumption records and drink types
  • Fitness Data: Activity data from connected wearable devices (with your consent)
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns to improve our services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Track and display your daily water intake progress
  • Provide personalized hydration recommendations
  • Sync data with connected fitness devices
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent for fitness device integration
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect our rights and safety

4. Fitness Device Integration

When you connect fitness devices (like Google Fit), we:

  • Only access activity data necessary for hydration recommendations
  • Store this data securely in our database
  • Allow you to disconnect devices at any time
  • Delete associated data when you disconnect

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure database storage
  • Regular security updates
  • Limited access to personal data

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@hydromate.app
Address: HydroMate Privacy Team
