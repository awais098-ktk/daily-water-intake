Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ current_date or "January 2025" }}

1. Information We Collect

HydroMate collects the following information to provide our water intake tracking service:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and profile data you provide during registration
  • Water Intake Data: Daily water consumption logs, hydration goals, and tracking history
  • Google Fit Data: Activity data, steps, calories burned, and fitness metrics (only when you connect Google Fit)
  • Usage Data: App interaction patterns, feature usage, and performance analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized hydration tracking and recommendations
  • Sync your activity data from Google Fit for better hydration insights
  • Generate progress reports and analytics
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send hydration reminders and notifications (if enabled)

3. Google Fit Integration

When you connect Google Fit:

  • We access your activity data (steps, calories, workouts) to provide personalized hydration recommendations
  • We do not store your Google account credentials
  • You can disconnect Google Fit at any time from the app settings
  • We only access data necessary for hydration recommendations

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on Microsoft Azure cloud infrastructure
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission and storage
  • Access to your data is restricted to essential app functionality only
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Disconnect third-party integrations (Google Fit)
  • Export your water intake data

6. Data Retention

  • We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • You can delete your account at any time
  • Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Anonymous usage analytics may be retained for app improvement

7. Third-Party Services

HydroMate integrates with:

  • Google Fit: For activity data synchronization (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Microsoft Azure: For secure cloud hosting and data storage

8. Children's Privacy

HydroMate is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or email.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us:

  • App: Use the in-app chatbot or contact form
  • GitHub: https://github.com/awais098-ktk/daily-water-intake
Your Privacy Matters

HydroMate is committed to protecting your privacy. We only collect data necessary to provide our hydration tracking service and never sell your personal information.
